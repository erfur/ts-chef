## 🚀 Overview

`ts-chef` brings the power of [GCHQ's CyberChef](https://github.com/gchq/CyberChef) into your favorite editor. Perform complex data transformations, decodings, and encodings without ever leaving your code.

Whether you're analyzing malware, debugging network protocols, or just need to pretty-print a messy JSON, `ts-chef` has you covered.

## ✨ Key Features

-   **480+ Operations:** Full range of decoders, encoders, ciphers, and hashing algorithms.
-   **Smart Scanning:** Automatically identifies patterns like Base64, Hex, or URLs in your selection.
-   **Pipeline Editor:** Build complex multi-step recipes with a modern UI.
-   **Real-time Preview:** See results instantly as you tweak your pipeline.
-   **Deep Analysis:** Deeply inspect data to uncover hidden layers of encoding.
-   **Variable Support:** Save intermediate results and use them as arguments in later steps.
